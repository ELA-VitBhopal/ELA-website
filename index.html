English Literary Association is the go to club for soft skills development. We firmly believe that mastering the soft skills is going to be the ace up your sleeves you will need to set yourself apart from the crowd. ELA is all set to offer you all a bunch of amazing events and fun trivia to enhance your levels of English in the most fun way out there. Our priority is to develop sensitization about improving our oration skills and confidence. We also aim to bring forth a community with increased involvement with each passing event. Allowing students to exercise their mental faculties and oratory skills is at the focal center of everything that we do in this club.
